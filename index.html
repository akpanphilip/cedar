Our Products

SATIN

Cedar Washable satin paint has a beautiful luster with a bit glossy finish, giving it a velvety appearance. It is suitable to be used on walls, ceilings and trims.

MATT

edar Matt is a premium, durable emulsion paint that provides a flat sheen finish, is resistant to salt and water, and is suitable for both interior and exterior surfaces.

EMULSION

Cedar Emulsion is a regular water-based emulsion paint, ideal for walls and ceilings, providing smooth and reliable coverage. It can be applied on both exterior and interior walls

TEXTURED

Cedar Texture Paint is a durable exterior wall paint specially formulated to withstand harsh tropical weather while protecting walls from mold and mildew growth.

TROWEL

Cedar Trowel is a heavy-bodied textured finish that protects walls and effectively masks imperfections, uneven surfaces, and irregularities in a single coat.

PK 90

Cedar PK 90 is a smooth, durable, ready-to-use screeding formula that evens masonry finishes and adds protection for both exterior and interior surfaces.

PRIMER

Cedar Primer is an easy-to-use water-based primer, fortified with fungicides, anti-damp, and anti-alkaline properties for superior wall protection.

GRAVITEX

Cedar Gravitex Paint is ideal for exterior and high-traffic areas, concealing masonry imperfections while providing a premium finish and long-lasting protection.

GLOSS

Cedar Gloss Paint is a durable, reflective finish known for hardness, washability, and dirt resistance, ideal for high-traffic areas like doors, cabinets, trim, and metals
